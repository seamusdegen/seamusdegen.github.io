About Our Team

Mako "Seamus Degen", Iron Vest "Anthony Silvestri", and TacticalWeasel1 "Hayden Zaverl" are the founding members of the Fe3 gaming team. Non competitive and non monetary, this team brings gaming content to the world, to bring entertainment and a good time for all that view. We are starting out slow and will post content to social media as quick as we can. All the content from this website and other social media outlets is used as a fan art and as a fan media page.
