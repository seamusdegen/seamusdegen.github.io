Modern Warfare DLC 1 Update

Modern Warfare has been out for a short time but we already have details for the new and upcoming dlc. The dlc will be released on December 3rd, along with patch notes. This update will have two new maps, three new game modes, and two new weapons. There will be two new spec ops missions along with three new classic spec ops missions to run with up to four people. The big update will be the intergration of a new battlepass system very similar to that of many other fps shooters on the market. Please stay tuned for more content when it is released.
